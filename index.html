Research

I work on Computational Photography and Generative Models for efficient, high-fidelity image generation. My previous work also includes End-to-end Optimization of ISPs (Image Signal Processing) and Opto-physical Simulations.

DualDn: Dual-domain Denoising via Differentiable ISP
Ruikang Li, Yujin Wang, Shiqi Chen, Fan Zhang, Jinwei Gu, Tianfan Xue
ECCV, 2024
project page / arXiv / supplement / code

We propose to jointly optimize denoisers in the RAW and sRGB domains bridged by a dedicated differentiable ISP. Once trained with synthetic noise, it can work on real noise for unseen smartphones and public benchmark.
Dark2Light: multi-stage progressive learning model for low-light image enhancement
Ruikang Li*, Menghao Li*, Shiqi Chen†, Yueting Chen, Zhihai Xu
Optics Express (OE), 2023
paper

We propose a multi-stage model for progressive low-light image restoration, disentangling illumination enhancement and noise removal within each stage to mitigate ISP-induced coupling.
Imaging Chain Modeling and a Scaling Experiment for Optical Remote Sensing of Lunar Surface
Menghao Li*, Ruikang Li*, Shiwei Bao, Zhihai Xu, Qi Li, Huajun Feng, Yueting Chen†, Hongyao Chen
IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement (TIM), 2024
paper

We present a physics-based optical imaging-chain simulation for remote sensing that models the full pipeline from 3D surface scattering to the camera's DV output via 3D structures, spectral properties, and calibration.
Optical Imaging Chain Modeling for the Permanently Shadowed Regions at Lunar South Pole
Menghao Li*, Jiapu Yan*, Ruikang Li*, Zhihai Xu†, Qi Li, Huajun Feng
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (TGRS), 2024
paper

We build a physics-based model tailored to the challenging illumination conditions of permanently shadowed regions, integrating terrain geometry, orbital configuration, camera parameters, and environmental settings.
